Who We Are

Your Instant Midwives

Tasmin & Phillipa

Between us, we bring over 31 years of midwifery experience and a shared belief that every woman deserves to feel heard, supported, and confident throughout her pregnancy journey.

Tasmin qualified as a midwife in 2011 and has since built a practice rooted in holistic, empowering care. Trained in hypnobirthing, hypnotherapy, Reiki, and aromatherapy in pregnancy, she brings a gentle, whole-person approach to everything she does. A mum of four and grandmother of two, Tasmin knows firsthand that the journey into motherhood isn't always straightforward. Having navigated postpartum depression herself, returned to study as a single parent to four children, and balanced the demands of home and work life with determination, she brings a depth of personal understanding that goes far beyond clinical training. That lived experience is woven into everything she does.

When she's not working, you'll find Tasmin on the dancefloor — salsa is her creative outlet and her freedom, a passion that reflects the same joy and expressiveness she brings to her practice. Her love of research, languages, and travel means she's always learning, always curious, and always bringing something new to the women she supports.

Phillipa has been working as a midwife since 2008, supporting women and families through some of the most important and life-changing moments of their lives. Over the years she has also qualified as a sonographer, allowing her to combine her passion for maternity care with specialist knowledge in ultrasound scanning. Her work is centred on providing compassionate, personalised care, and she believes every woman deserves to feel informed, heard, and empowered throughout her pregnancy and childbirth journey.

As both a healthcare professional and a mum of two, Phillipa brings not only clinical experience but also personal understanding to the care she provides. She knows how emotional, exciting, and sometimes overwhelming pregnancy and parenthood can be, which is why empathy is at the heart of everything she does. She is passionate about advocating for women, ensuring they feel supported in making choices that are right for them, while offering the reassurance of up-to-date knowledge in pregnancy, childbirth, and ultrasound care.

Outside of her professional life, Phillipa enjoys making the most of family time and the simple pleasures that bring balance to a busy career. She loves travelling and exploring new places, whether it's a weekend getaway or a bigger adventure abroad, and enjoys hiking, socialising with friends and family, relaxing with Netflix, and discovering new restaurants and great food.

Her goal has always been to combine professional expertise with warmth and genuine connection, creating a caring and supportive experience for every family she works alongside.

Together, we created My Instant Midwife to make expert midwifery support accessible to every woman, wherever she is and whenever she needs it.

MIM is an acronym of My Instant Midwife — but it means a little more than that. Tasmin's grandson Oscar called her Mimmy when he was small, and as he grew, it became simply MIM. A name born from family, worn with pride, and now at the heart of everything we do.

We can't wait to walk alongside you.
