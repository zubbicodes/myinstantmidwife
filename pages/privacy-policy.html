My Instant Midwife (MIM) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with the care and confidentiality it deserves. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

My Instant Midwife (MIM) is the data controller responsible for your personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us at support@mimmyinstantmidwife.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following information:

Personal Information

  • Full name, date of birth, and contact details
  • Billing address and payment information
  • Email address and phone number

Health and Pregnancy Information

  • Medical history relevant to your pregnancy
  • Pregnancy details including gestational age and due date
  • Information about your antenatal care and healthcare providers
  • Any health concerns or symptoms you share with us

Communication Data

  • WhatsApp messages exchanged with your MIM midwife
  • Records of scheduled calls and video consultations
  • Email correspondence

Technical Data

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and usage data relating to our website

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Deliver the midwifery support services you have subscribed to
  • Manage your account, billing, and subscription
  • Communicate with you about your care, appointments, and service updates
  • Provide personalised educational content tailored to your stage of pregnancy
  • Comply with our professional obligations as NMC-registered midwives
  • Improve our services and ensure their safety and quality

4. Our Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract: To deliver the services you have subscribed to
  • Legal obligation: To comply with our professional and regulatory duties as NMC-registered midwives
  • Legitimate interests: To manage and improve our service safely and effectively
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly agreed to specific uses of your data, such as marketing communications
Health data is classified as special category data under UK GDPR. We process this data on the basis of providing healthcare and under our professional obligations as registered midwives.

5. How We Share Your Data

MIM will never sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Mailchimp: For delivery of daily emails and service communications
  • Professional obligation: Where we are legally or professionally required to share information, for example in the interests of safeguarding
  • Legal requirement: Where disclosure is required by law or regulatory authority

All third-party processors are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with UK GDPR.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and health data for a minimum of eight years following the end of your subscription, in line with NHS and NMC record-keeping standards. Data relating to children is retained until the child's 25th birthday, or 26th birthday if the young person was 17 at the conclusion of care.

After the applicable retention period, your data will be securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure of your data, subject to our professional and legal retention obligations
  • Restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability of your data in a commonly used format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@mimmyinstantmidwife.com

We will respond within 30 days.
